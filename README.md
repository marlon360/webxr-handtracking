1.  Use a supported device (e.g. Oculus Quest)

    1.  Visit [chrome://flags/](chrome://flags/)
    2.  Enable _WebXR experiences with joints tracking (#webxr-hands)_

4.  Enable "Auto Enable Hands or Controllers" (Quest Settings (Gear Icon) -> Device -> Hands and Controllers)
5.  Open an example from [https://webxr-handtracking.vercel.app](https://webxr-handtracking.vercel.app)
6.  Enter the VR experience

    1. If you have _WebXR experiences with hands tracking (#webxr-hands-tracking)_ enabled, you can start the VR experience with your hands
    2. If you have _WebXR experiences with hands tracking (#webxr-hands-tracking)_ disabled, use your controllers (not your hand) to enter the VR experience after that put the controllers down
